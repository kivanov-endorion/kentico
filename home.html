Commerce & Lifecycle Services

In line with our clear strategy we deliver lifecycle services along with a wide range of supporting technology. Thanks to our diverse range of services we can offer a unique, specialized set of options to meet your needs and those of your end customer.

  • Reverse Logistics & Repair
  • Insurance Management

  • ODM supply
  • IT Asset Disposition
